2 DAYS MOON-WALKING TOUR

Date: May 5th-8th, 2066

Distance: 384,400KM

Difficulty:

This experience tour is designed for first-time space travelers. In collaboration with NASA. Guided by experienced astronauts and trainers, travel back and forth to the lunar space station on the DRAGON spacecraft developed by SPACE X. Take a spacewalk, collect meteorites, and experience the human feat of half a century ago.

Itinerary:

  • Visit NASA, experience weightlessness training.
  • Enter the designated orbit of 34sP* and enjoy the view of the Earth.
  • Roam the moon, visit the landing site and collect meteorite fragments.

MARS STAYCATION

Date: DEC, 2065

Distance: 5,4000,000KM

Difficulty:

Mars, with its Earth-like environment and abundant resources, has become a possible destination for human habitation. This month-long journey takes you to China's Mars base to observe missions including water purification and search for Martian life. Suitable for those with a strong interest in science.

Itinerary:

  • Ride the Mars Ascent Vehicle to ascend Mount 75FF#.
  • Produce drinking water using the fuel "hydrazine" and participate in space planting.
  • Follow the Mars base research team deep underground to explore the footprints of alien life.
